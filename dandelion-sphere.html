About

About Dandelion Sphere

Dandelion Sphere began with a fascination for a brief and fragile moment in nature - a form so delicate that it exists only for an instant before disappearing. By preserving this moment in resin, it becomes something lasting, contemplative, and quietly extraordinary.

A preserved moment

Each piece is made by hand, with patience and precision. The process respects the individuality of the dandelion itself, allowing nature to remain at the centre of the object.

The beauty of a dandelion lies not only in its form, but in what it suggests - transience, possibility, lightness, and change. Dandelion Sphere turns that passing moment into an object that can remain in a room for years.

Handmade at Cedar House

A slower way of making.

Created in a setting shaped by stillness and landscape, each sphere reflects a slower way of making. The work is careful, tactile, and deeply focused on material, light, and proportion.
